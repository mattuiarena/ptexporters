Who We Are

Founded by Mr.Vijayakumar in 2017, PT Expoters has been a key player in the export/import business in South India. The company is based on South India and handling world-wide shipment on regular basis. The company have major connections on almost all the port agencies around the world.

The company exports and Imports oil products at first from middle east to south India and expands its horizon over the time. Now the company handles large quatitles of oil products, spices and even has its signature on the software products the is exported from south India.

History

The company was founded on 2017 and ever since its showing a steady and progressive growth over time. During the course of its growth the company picks up the most important shipping agency tie up and import/export partners.

The company has being partnering up with the major players in oil industry like lukoil and more for its seemless operation around the globe. The company has its branches in South India, Dubai and in Madagascar.
